Trust Equation

McKnight & Chervany present us a equation that is useful to calculate the trust on an specific situation, in specific moments based in some variables that represent all those factor that we have to know.

The Challenge.

In a car-sharing situation we had to evaluate the variables of the equation. In this scenario we can find two trust situations, one for the driver and other for the person that needs this service.

Investigation Process.

The Solution.

Mobile app for car sharing where passengers and drivers can find the more trustable escenario for them.
